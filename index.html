Tournament starts in

MPL 2019...


India is a vast country with people belonging to different religions,languages,taste, Preferences but the one thing that uniting this country together and that is cricket. Being a B-School we have tried to combine students passion for cricket and their Management knowledge,and have come up with MPL (MBA Premier League) which is combination of both.

This one of a kind sporting league’s aim is to:

#Focus on sports management as an industry through MPL
#Test business knowledge as well as sporting skills in a single event
#Create a networking and idea exchange platform for B-schools
#Engage multiple skills and challenges to create a sense of teamspirit camaraderie.
#Provide intellectual simulations along with spreading a message of individual health and well-being.





About Us...

In 2006, IEIBS Akademia initiated the idea of supporting Stakeholders (Corporates, Institutes, Students Government) of The MBA industry. Over the years Akademia has supported 300+ students yearly along with our 5 associations (Partner Institutes) all over Maharashtra. Knowledge partner to these five Institutes, Akademia specializes in transforming young aspirants into world leaders. IEIBS Akademia-AS Snow stands as the sole representative from India that has become an institutional member at CEEMAN. Mr. Nilesh Sarawate (CEO IEIBS Akademia) has been the chief protagonist in the concept and has mentored many students, Anmol Ambani (S/O'Anil Ambani) being one of the many, throughout his career & continues to do so. With mentors like Mrs. Munira Lokhandwala (A five-time CAT Topper & an Alumni of IIM Kolkata), Mr. Pawan Agarwal (CEO-Pathfinder Corporates, an Alumnus IIM Kolkata IIT Kanpur) & Mrs. Arpita Sur (Senior Decision Scientist at Ugam, An Alumni IIM-Kolkata IIT Bombay), IEIBS Akademia is assisting the aspirants to discover their potentials and achieve a successful career. Akademia initiates yet another concept through MPL by creating a stage for aspirants to showcase their talent in sports and management providing them with an opportunity to take their skills to the corporate level.
